Language Generator

Algorithmically generate fictional language for less than 30 seconds.

How to use:

  1. Select ingredient(s) by clicking at the language family name (Austronesian etc.) then click 'Equip'.
  2. A slider will appear. You can drag each slider to configure how much a language family affects the fctional language you'll make.
  3. Set how long you wish the algorithm takes to generate language. Even the longest still take shorter than 30 seconds.
  4. The resulting sentence of your fictional language will appear at the rightmost section of the site.
